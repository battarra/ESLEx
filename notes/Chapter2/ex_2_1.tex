\exercise{2.1}

Since $||t_k||^2 = 1$, one has:
\begin{eqnarray}
||t_k - \hat{y}||^2 = 1 - 2 \hat{y}_k + ||\hat{y}||^2
\end{eqnarray}
hence:
\begin{eqnarray}
\textrm{argmin}_k ||t_k - \hat{y}|| = \textrm{argmin}_k ||t_k - \hat{y}||^2 = 
\textrm{argmax}_k\, \hat{y}_k
\end{eqnarray}
